We are LocalDeal. Everything you can buy, anywhere, under one roof. Your one stop business directory.

FOR BUSINESS

Local Deal is an online business directory that allows businesses to advertise themselves to a broader target market, those interested in the product or service being offered as well as the opportunity to just grab the attention of other potential clients who see an advertisement that intrigues them. At Local Deal we focus on personal touch and that is whereby each business onboarded will have an assigned support personnel at Local Deal. Our support personnel will upload your business onto the Local Deal website, and you will be able to contact them twice a month with any discounts, promotions or interesting news which you may want to make known to the public. With this we are able to keep our users up to date with the most recent deals being run by businesses as well as allow people to search for everything from A-Z on one platform. In addition, the platform also enables users to look at competition and see which deal suits them best.
Furthermore, Local Deal allows freelancers and other services on the platform. We are focused on all sectors, every business – no matter the size. Whichever product or service you are offering; we will advertise it to a greater client base

FOR THE PUBLIC

We are a website-based platform that allows people to view, without signing up, all businesses within a specific area to the liking of the person. This means that if the person is looking to search for a restaurant shop within 8 km from where they are – they can be so specific. Local Deal allows you to search for literally any product/service that comes to mind. You are also able to look at the product which you have searched and then look at the different businesses offering it to differentiate between the offerings and ratings. You will be presented with the relevant information to of the business to proceed with your purchase.
